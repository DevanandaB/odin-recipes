Million Dollar Spaghetti

Here's how to make some delicious pasta in 10 steps or less!

Million dollar spaghetti is the perfect combination of noodles, lean ground beef sauce, cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream. It's unbelievably good!

This delicious spaghetti dish features a beefy, creamy tomato sauce that’s impossible to resist.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti: An 8-ounce package of spaghetti should make about eight servings.
  • Beef: You’ll need a pound of lean ground beef.
  • Sauce A jar of store-brought sauce or make your own homemade sauce if you know how to.
  • Butter
  • Cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream
  • Salt
  • Cheese: Cheddar cheese is the best for this recipe!

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until cooked, about 8 to 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Cook the beef on the stove for about 5-8 minutes and discard the grease.
  4. Transfer the beef to a bowl and mix in the spaghetti sauce.
  5. Prepare the baking dish, then spread 1/2 of the butter slices and spread 1/2 of the pasta into the dish.
  6. Mix cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream together in a bowl; spread mixture over spaghetti.
  7. Cover with remaining spaghetti and top with remaining slices of butter.
  8. Pour ground beef mixture over spaghetti and spread in an even layer.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Spread Cheddar cheese on top and continue baking until cheese has melted and is lightly browned, about 15 minutes more.
  10. Enjoy!
